Design Principles Document

Jehmel C. Ofreneo

Alignment

Shopify

shopify.com

The webpage shows left aligment of text above the images. The images are aligned horizontally and the text below them are aligned with respect to the left side of the images.

Contrast

CapRocat

caprocat.com

The webpage shows the dark green color of the bushes on the plant boxes in contrast to the bright colors of the castle walls and the pathway towards the entrance. The white text is readable in contrast to the background.

Repetition

CIT

CIT.com

The size of the images as well as the text and icons in the middle of the images are consistently repeated creating a uniform look of the items displayed.
