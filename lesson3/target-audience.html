Target Audience:

A lot of people live, work, study, and visit in the community. They make use of the internet through computers and mobile devices to update themselves of news, events, activities, and the weather. Residents of the place want to know if it is a good day to do laundry, to go to market, to do gardening, or to engage in leisure activities. Professionals want to get as much information as to what is happening in the industry and the local economy. Students need to get information of activities they can go to or join after school or on weekends and visitors want to get information to help them make the most out a possible travel to the place and avoid having their time wasted.

Personas:

Jose Vargas

Jose Vargas is 42 years old and a civil engineer as well as a family man. He has two sons in senior high school and one daughter in junior high, and one in the elementary. Before going to work, he needs to drop off his kids at their respective schools. His wife stays home most of the time but has to schedule her time to go pay the bills, go to buy food at the local market or the mall to make purchases for supplies needed for the home. She avoids going out when the weather is rainy or stormy.

Dorothy Chan

Dorothy Chan is 29 years old, unmarried and a business woman who loves to travel around and visit different places. She has a lot of relatives scattered around the country and she makes sure to visit them when she is around their places. She dislikes traveling in bad weather conditions unless she really has to do it.

Senarios:

  • Jose wakes up early on a Monday morning. While eating breakfast, he browses the internet for the local news and the weather. He is glad that the weather will be fine for the week and decides to leave early with the children so that he will be early for work.
  • The weather is stormy. Jose decides to work the whole day at the office and he cancels his planned visit on the sites of two projects he is presently supervising.
  • Dorothy postpones her visit to the place because she found out through the internet of repairs being done on the main highway due to a flood that was caused by continuous heavy rain during the past two days.
  • Jose tells her wife to go to the market because a storm was due to come within two days. She has to buy enough food to last for a week after which the storm would have already left.
  • Dorothy finds out while browsing the phone that a coming event that businessmen will be gathering at a local hotel venue. Since the weather will be fine for the whole week, she plans to come a day early to attend the meeting to visit with some close friends who will be part of the event.
